Motorcycle

摩托 Motorcycle

风声、引擎和公路，是另一种把身体交给世界的方式。 Wind, engine, and road become another way of giving the body back to the world.

1 / 4

跨上机车，把风声当作配乐、把公路当作画布，在引擎的轰鸣与车轮的前行中感受自由最直接的模样。 Mount the motorcycle, treat the wind as music, and see the road as a canvas, feeling the most direct form of freedom in the roar of the engine and the forward motion of the wheels.

艺术 / 点滴 Art / Fragments
